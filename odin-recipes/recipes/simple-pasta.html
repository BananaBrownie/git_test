Simple pasta

simple easy pasta with a sprinkle of parmesan cheese and cilantro

15 minute 6 ingredient pasta, easy,simple and affordable to make as an every day meal!

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces spaghetti
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and ground black pepper

Steps

  1. boil your pasta until desired texture.
  2. place olive oil on pan
  3. put garlic in pan and cook until golden
  4. place pasta with 1/2 of pasta water in pan
  5. place garlic and salt in pan with pasta
  6. cook and mix for 3 minutes
  7. turn off hear and add parmesan and parsley
