Apple pie

Apple pie,great to eat with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

For this recipe we're going to need 9 basic ingredients, we're also going to need dough to make the crust and top of the pie.

Ingredients

  • apple pie crust
  • 4 medium size apples
  • 50g sugar
  • 50g brown sugar
  • juice of 1/2 a lemon
  • 1 tbp of cinnamon
  • 1/4 tbp nutmeg powder
  • a pinch of salt
  • 1 tbp of baking flour
  • 2 tbp of butter

Steps

  1. neat your pie dough
  2. With clean hands cut your apples into slices
  3. place apple slices in a bowl and add your sugars in and mix together with your hand
  4. add your lemon juice,cinnamon,nutmeg, and baking flour mix until incorporated
  5. butter your pie mold
  6. place apple slices in dough thats already places in your pie mold
