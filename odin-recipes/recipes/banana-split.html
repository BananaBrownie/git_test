Banana Split

A classic banana split ideal for the unbearable summer months

For out banana split recipe we're going to need store bought ingredients that we'll then slap together, or gently place together to make our classic banana split.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • chocolate ice cream
  • vanilla ice cream
  • strawberry ice cream
  • chopped nuts
  • whip cream
  • cherries
  • chocolate syrup

Steps

  1. slice banana in half the long way.
  2. scoop out one scoop of each flavor of ice cream
  3. place scoops of ice cream on plate
  4. place sliced banana on both sides of your ice cream
  5. add whip cream, nuts, chocolate syrup and finally your cherry
  6. Enjoy before it melts!
