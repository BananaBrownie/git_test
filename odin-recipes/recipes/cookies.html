Cookies

The best recipe for hot and sexy homemade cookies.

These homemade, homegrown cookies are made from scratch, so get ready to wow your loved ones with this simple,but delicious recipe.

Ingredients

  • 225g butter
  • 110g sugar
  • 275g flour
  • 1tsp cinnamon
  • 75g dark chocolate chips

Steps

  1. 1. Mix butter in bowl until soft
  2. Mix sugar into butter until creamy
  3. Sift flour and cinnamon
  4. Freeze dough for 15 minutes
  5. Make dough into palm sized balls,place on baking sheet and flatten dough
  6. Bake until you see golden brown edges
